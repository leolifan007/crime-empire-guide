Opium is the most reliable early-game income source in DDS2. Here is how to set up a production line that funds your entire operation.

## Where to Grow

The northern farm hideout has the best soil quality. Purchase it for $4,500 as soon as you complete the tutorial.

## Growing Cycle

| Stage | Duration | Action Required |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| Planting | Day 1 | Sow seeds in prepared soil |
| Growth | Days 2-5 | Water daily, check for pests |
| Harvest | Day 6 | Collect raw opium sap |
| Processing | Day 7 | Cook into smokeable form |

## Optimal Setup

Plant 6 poppies per cycle. Each mature plant yields 3 units of raw opium. A full cycle of 6 plants = 18 units = $2,700 profit at market rate.

## Warning

Do not store raw opium at your main hideout. Police raids target production sites. Keep raw materials at the farm and only move finished product to distribution.
