If your first meth cook in DDS2 ended with an explosion, you are not alone. The game's chemistry system punishes guesswork.

## Step 1: Get the Recipe

Break into the warehouse marked on your map during the tutorial. The recipe is on the desk.

## Step 2: Gather Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Where to Find |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Pseudoephedrine | 10 units | Pharmacy (night) |
| Anhydrous Ammonia | 5 units | Farm supply |
| Lithium | 3 units | Battery section |

## Step 3: Set Up Your Lab

Place equipment in this order: Hot plate -> Beaker -> Condenser. Wrong order = failed cook.

## Step 4: The Cook

Heat to exactly 175F. Hold for 3 minutes. Cool rapidly. If the mixture turns yellow, you overcooked it -- start over.

## Community Tip

Use the parkour shortcut behind the pharmacy to avoid police patrols when gathering ingredients. Saves 5 minutes per run.
