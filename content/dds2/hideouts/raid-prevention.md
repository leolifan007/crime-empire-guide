{{< callout "info" >}}
Getting raided in DDS2 is not a matter of if, but when. The average player loses their main hideout 3-4 times before learning proper countermeasures. This guide covers raid prediction, prevention, and disaster recovery.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "How to Predict a Raid 24 Hours Before" >}}

The game gives you subtle cues before a raid happens. Most players miss them:

| Cue | Timing | What It Means |
|-----|--------|---------------|
| Suspicious van parked nearby | 24 hrs before | Hideout is being scouted |
| Unknown caller on ShadyComm | 12 hrs before | Warning from inside contact |
| Unusual police patrol patterns | 6 hrs before | Area is being surveyed |
| "Strange smell" notification | 3 hrs before | Police K-9 unit inbound |

**Action on any cue:** Move 80% of your product to an alternate hideout immediately. Keep only what you need for the next 24 hours of orders.

{{< callout "warning" >}}The Suspicious Van is the most reliable indicator. If you see a white panel van within 2 blocks of your hideout that was not there before, it is a scout. Mark its location on your map and check if it moves or stays. Stationary van = active surveillance.{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "During a Raid: Survival Protocol" >}}

If you are present during a raid:

1. **Do not fight.** Exit through the back or escape tunnel
2. **Do not grab items.** Every second counts
3. **Do not return for 24 hours.** Police stakeout lasts 1 in-game day
4. **Check which hideout was hit** (notification includes location)

If you are not present: the raid resolves automatically based on guard presence and door upgrades.

| Setup | Outcome |
|-------|---------|
| No guard, basic door | 80% loss |
| 1 guard, basic door | 40% loss |
| 1 guard, reinforced door | 20% loss |
| 2 guards, reinforced + hiding compartment | 5% loss |

{{< section "Post-Raid Recovery Plan" >}}

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| Immediately | Reload autosave (if within 3 min of raid start) |
| Hour 1 | Transfer remaining assets to undamaged hideouts |
| Hour 2 | Buy guard for the hit hideout |
| Day 1 | Restock raw materials from suppliers |
| Day 2 | Resume production at alternate lab |
| Day 3-5 | Rebuild to pre-raid capacity |

{{< insight >}}The autosave trick works because the game autosaves on hideout entry. If you entered your hideout and immediately triggered a raid notification, the autosave happened before the raid. Reloading puts you outside the hideout with 100% of your assets intact.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Raid-Proofing Checklist" >}}

- [ ] Each hideout has at least 1 guard
- [ ] No single hideout holds >30% of net worth
- [ ] Cash split across 3+ locations
- [ ] Escape route memorized (2 exits)
- [ ] Hiding compartment installed on main production lab
- [ ] Reinforced doors on any hideout with >$10k value
- [ ] "Emergency transfer" stash location within 30 sec run of each hideout
