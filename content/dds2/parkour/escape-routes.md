{{< callout "info" >}}
A DDS2 police chase typically lasts 90-120 seconds. Your goal is not to outrun the police -- it is to break line of sight and enter a hiding spot before they triangulate your position. These 5 escape routes cover every district.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Route 1: Market District Roof Run" >}}

**Best for:** Losing heat after a delivery gone wrong in Pueblo

```
Start: Market center
  1. Sprint north toward church
  2. Vault market stall (crouch immediately)
  3. Wall run right at the blue building corner
  4. Jump climb to awning (first roof level)
  5. Cross to church roof (2x sprint jump)
  6. Drop into cemetery behind church
  7. Hide behind large tombstone (15 sec)
  
Police lost: ~45 seconds
```

**Skill requirement:** Low. Only requires basic vault + jump climb. The church roof gap is the hardest part.

---

**Route 2: Playa Hotel Escape**

**Best for:** Losing heat during red-level pursuit

```
Start: Hotel Playa front entrance
  1. Enter hotel, sprint to staircase
  2. Climb to 3rd floor
  3. Exit to balcony (east side)
  4. Wall run along balcony railing
  5. Jump to adjacent building roof
  6. Cross 2 roof gaps
  7. Ladder down into alley
  8. Hide in green dumpster
  
Police lost: ~60 seconds
```

**Skill requirement:** Medium. The wall run + jump gap is tight. Miss it and you fall to street level.

---

**Route 3: Industrial Zone Pipe Slide**

**Best for:** Quick escape from Warehouse B area

```
Start: Warehouse B loading dock
  1. Vault north fence
  2. Sprint to factory wall
  3. Wall run left (3 seconds)
  4. Jump climb to exposed pipe
  5. Slide down pipe (covers 3 floors)
  6. Sprint to train car #042
  7. Enter train car, close door
  
Police lost: ~35 seconds
```

**Skill requirement:** Low. The pipe slide is forgiving and the train car is a full hiding spot (safe save).

---

**Route 4: North Farms Barn Escape**

**Best for:** Rural zone evasion

```
Start: North Farm shack
  1. Exit through back window (vault)
  2. Sprint to hay bale stack
  3. Jump climb to top of hay
  4. Wall run across barn wall
  5. Vault into barn loft window
  6. Hide in hay loft
  
Police lost: ~25 seconds
```

**Skill requirement:** Very Low. The hay bales are easy to climb. Best beginner escape route.

---

**Route 5: Downtown Rooftop Sprint**

**Best for:** Endgame high-value district

```
Start: Downtown apartment
  1. Exit to fire escape
  2. Climb to roof (4 flights)
  3. Sprint across 3 connected roofs
  4. Vault air conditioning unit
  5. Wall run across narrow gap (hardest part)
  6. Enter office building via roof door
  7. Navigate to basement parking
  8. Exit through maintenance tunnel
  
Police lost: ~90 seconds
```

**Skill requirement:** High. Requires precise wall-run timing. Practice in single-player before attempting with product.

{{< section "Escape Route Selection Matrix" >}}

| Situation | Recommended Route | Reasoning |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Just did a deal, mild heat | Route 1 (Market) | Quick, low skill |
| Red pursuit, any district | Route 2 (Hotel) | Reliable line-of-sight break |
| In lab with product | Route 3 (Industrial) | Fast exit, saves product |
| Rural area ambush | Route 4 (Farms) | Easiest execution |
| Downtown, high net worth | Route 5 (Downtown) | Most thorough escape |

{{< callout "warning" >}}Route 5 (Downtown) should only be used if you are confident in wall-run mechanics. A failed wall run in Downtown drops you into the highest police density zone on the map. If you are not ready, use Route 2 as a fallback.{{< /callout >}}
