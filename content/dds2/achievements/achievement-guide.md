{{< callout "info" >}}
Drug Dealer Simulator 2 has 39 Steam achievements. About 20 are earned through natural progression. The remaining 19 require specific actions that most players never discover. This guide covers every achievement with unlock conditions, including the 5 missable ones.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Story Progression Achievements (Auto-Unlock)" >}}

These 12 achievements unlock naturally as you progress through the main missions:

| Achievement | Unlock Condition | Notes |
|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Welcome to Isla Sombra | Complete tutorial | Unmissable |
| First Deal | Make your first street sale | Unmissable |
| ShadyComm Apprentice | Complete 10 ShadyComm orders | ~30 min play |
| Lab Rat | Set up your first lab | After getting recipe |
| Cookin' | Complete first successful cook | After finding recipe |
| Staff Up | Hire your first employee | Any staff type |
| Territory Claimed | Buy your first territory expansion | Costs $8k+ |
| Rank Up (Rank 2) | Reach rank 2 | Natural progression |
| Rank Up (Rank 3) | Reach rank 3 | Natural progression |
| Rank Up (Rank 4) | Reach rank 4 | Natural progression |
| Rank Up (Rank 5) | Reach rank 5 | Natural progression |
| The Big Time | Reach rank 6 | Takes 80+ hours |

{{< section "Missable Achievements" >}}

These 5 achievements can be permanently missed if you progress past certain points:

| Achievement | Miss Window | How to Get |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Tutoral Master | Complete all tutorial objectives | Must do ALL optional tutorial steps before leaving Pueblo |
| First Love | Complete Ruby's questline before rank 3 | Ruby is in the tutorial zone. Speak to her at night. |
| Museum Heist | Steal the golden statue before the museum upgrades security | Museum is only unguarded for first 30 in-game days |
| Ghost | Complete the hidden laboratory quest (underground bunker west of Industrial Zone) | Requires finding the hidden key in the church basement |
| Speed Demon | Finish any delivery in under 30 seconds from acceptance | Accept a Zone 1 order, pre-position near the delivery point |

{{< insight >}}The "Tutorial Master" achievement is the most commonly missed. The tutorial has 3 optional objectives hidden in unmarked locations: finding the waterfall safe, talking to the fisherman, and opening the locked chest behind the market. If you skip any of these before leaving Pueblo, the achievement is locked permanently for that save.{{< /insight >}}
