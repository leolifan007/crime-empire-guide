{{< section "Rarest Achievement Strategies" >}}

**Side hustle (0.6%):** The lowest unlock rate on Steam. Likely requires completing a specific set of optional missions or reaching a high threshold of side content.

**500 CIGARETTES (0.7%):** Requires selling 500 cigarette units. This is a grind -- cigarettes are low-value items that require many deliveries.

**Environmentalist (12.6%):** Sell 100 glass bottles. The most accessible rare achievement. Collect bottles from deliveries and vendor interactions.
