{{< callout "info" >}}
Crime Simulator supports up to 4-player co-op. Playing with friends doubles loot capacity, halves completion time, and unlocks heists that are impossible solo. But uncoordinated teams fail faster than solo players. Here is how to run an efficient crew.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Role Assignments for a 4-Player Team" >}}

| Role | Responsibility | Required Skills |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Infiltrator | Entry, lockpicking, camera disabling | Lockpicking 3, Silent Movement |
| Enforcer | Combat, guard elimination, intimidation | Combatives 3, Silenced Weapons |
| Transporter | Loot carrying, vehicle escape, extraction | Driving 2, Backpack (Duffel+) |
| Overwatch | Surveillance, radio coordination, backup | None (entry role, learn by watching) |

**For 2-player teams:** Combine Infiltrator + Enforcer (one player), Transporter + Overwatch (one player).

{{< insight >}}The most common co-op mistake is everyone trying to do everything. Designate clear roles before each job. The Infiltrator enters first, marks guard positions on the team map, then the Enforcer moves in. The Transporter only enters after all guards are down. This flow cuts heist time by 40%.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Co-op Mission Types" >}}

### Bank Heist (4 players recommended)

| Phase | Duration | Assigned Role | Action |
|-------|----------|--------------|--------|
| 1. Recon | 5 min | Overwatch | Mark all cameras and guard patrols |
| 2. Entry | 3 min | Infiltrator | Unlock side door, disable camera system |
| 3. Clear | 5 min | Enforcer | Stealth eliminate 4 guards |
| 4. Loot | 8 min | Transporter | Drill safe (3 min), carry loot to van |
| 5. Escape | 2 min | All | Fight through arriving backup, extract |

### Police Station Evidence Room (3 players minimum)

| Phase | Duration | Assigned Role | Action |
|-------|----------|--------------|--------|
| 1. Distraction | 2 min | Enforcer | Trigger alarm on opposite side of building |
| 2. Infiltration | 10 min | Infiltrator | Enter during chaos, reach evidence locker |
| 3. Extraction | 3 min | Transporter | Grab evidence (valuable), exit through roof |

### Warehouse Takeover (2-4 players)

| Phase | Duration | Assigned Role | Action |
|-------|----------|--------------|--------|
| 1. Clear exterior | 3 min | Enforcer + Infiltrator | Eliminate 2 exterior guards |
| 2. Secure interior | 5 min | Enforcer | Clear 3-4 interior guards |
| 3. Claim territory | 1 min | All | Interact with control terminal |
| 4. Defend | 10 min | All | Survive 2 waves of enemy reinforcements |

{{< section "Communication Protocols" >}}

| Callout Phrase | Meaning | Response |
|----------------|---------|----------|
| "Guard at [location]" | NPC in sight | Mark on map (Y key) |
| "Moving" | Changing position | "Copy" (acknowledge) |
| "Contact" | Engagement imminent | "Hold" (wait for signal) |
| "Clean" | Area is secure | "Moving in" (advance) |
| "Heat" | Police or backup incoming | "Evac" (extract immediately) |

Establish these before the job starts. Voice comms are strongly recommended -- text chat during stealth sections is too slow.

{{< section "Loot Sharing" >}}

Co-op missions generate shared loot. The game does not split it automatically.

| Method | How | Fairness |
|--------|-----|----------|
| Equal split | Manual count after mission | Highest control |
| Turn system | Alternate who takes the high-value item | Simple but imprecise |
| Mission leader | Host takes 60%, crew splits 40% | Common in pickup groups |
| Split by role | Infiltrator 30%, Enforcer 30%, Transporter 25%, Overwatch 15% | Rewards risk |

{{< callout "info" >}}For regular groups, the "equal split" method is simplest. Count total loot value, divide by player count. If one player takes a $800 gold watch and another takes $600 in electronics, the third gets $500 cash to balance. Spreadsheet helps for large hauls.{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Co-op Skill Synergies" >}}

Certain skill combinations multiply team effectiveness:

| Combo | Skills Required | Effect |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| Ghost Team | Infiltrator (Stealth 3) + Enforcer (Stealth 2) | Both can stealth kill, zero detection |
| Fast Heist | Infiltrator (Lockpick 3) + Transporter (Driving 2) | Entry in 30s, escape in 20s |
| Tank Squad | All players (Combatives 2+) | Can brute force most combat encounters |
| Scout Network | Overwatch (Driving 1 + any Stealth) | Quick recon + fast extraction |

**Synergy strategy:** Match your combo to the heist type. Ghost Team for Museum, Fast Heist for Bank, Tank Squad for Warehouse Takeover.
