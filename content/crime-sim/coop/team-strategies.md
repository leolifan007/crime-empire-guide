{{< callout "info" >}}
Good co-op in Crime Simulator is about synchronization. A team that executes together can clear a bank vault in 8 minutes. A team that runs individually dies in 2 minutes. These advanced tactics separate pro crews from random matchmaking disasters.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "The Bait and Switch (2 Players)" >}}

**Best for:** Break In missions with 2+ guards.

| Phase | Player 1 (Baiter) | Player 2 (Infiltrator) |
|-------|------------------|----------------------|
| 1 | Exposed position at front entrance | Hidden at side entrance |
| 2 | Throw rock/use noise maker at front door | Wait for guard investigation |
| 3 | When guard approaches, sprint away (drawing chase) | Enter through now-unlocked side door |
| 4 | Lose guard in alley (use parkingour/smoke) | Complete objective, exit |
| 5 | Regroup at extraction point | Carry loot to escape vehicle |

**Key timing:** Player 1 must survive 45-60 seconds. Anyone playing Bait should carry 2 Smoke Bombs and have Combatives 2 for emergency self-defense.

---

**The Overwatch Tower (3+ Players)**

**Best for:** Eliminate Target missions in open areas.

| Role | Position | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| Spotter | High vantage (roof/water tower) | Uses camera to track target and guards |
| Sniper | Adjacent roof, 80-100m | Silenced hunting rifle, waits for shot call |
| Getaway | Vehicle near extraction point | Engine running, exit route planned |

**Execution:** Spotter marks target and confirms no witnesses in kill zone. Sniper has 3-second window. If clean, Sniper takes shot and immediately retreats to extraction. If compromised, Getaway drives to Sniper's position while Spotter provides cover fire.

---

**The Two-Stage Heist (4 Players)**

**Best for:** Bank Vault and Museum heists.

```
Stage 1 (5 min):
  Team Alpha (Infiltrator + Overwatch): Clear ground floor, disable cameras
  Team Bravo (Enforcer + Transporter): Clear basement, prep drill
  
Stage 2 (8 min):
  Team Alpha: Move to vault, provide covering positions
  Team Bravo: Drill vault (3 min), load loot (5 min)
  
Extraction (2 min):
  All: Fight to vehicle, escape
```

{{< section "Voice Comms Cheat Sheet" >}}

```
[Starting phase]
Leader: "Positions. Everyone check in."
Team: "Alpha set." / "Bravo set." / "Charlie set."

[Execution phase]
Spotter: "Target is [location]. Wait for my mark."
Infiltrator: "Cameras down at [time]. Moving to objective."
Enforcer: "Two guards neutralized. Sector clear."

[Complication]
Any: "Contact! [Number] guards, [direction]."
Leader: "Alpha handle it. Bravo continue objective."

[Extraction]
Leader: "Extraction in 60 seconds. Complete and move."
Team: "Alpha extracting." (repeat for each team)
```

{{< section "Failed Run Recovery Protocol" >}}

| Situation | Action | Timing |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| One player arrested | Others complete objective, meet at safe house | Within 2 min |
| Alarm triggered (before objective) | Abort mission, split up, meet at safe house B | Immediately |
| Alarm triggered (after objective) | All players fight to extraction vehicle | Within 30s |
| Extraction vehicle destroyed | Run to nearest secondary extraction (marked on map) | Within 60s |
| 3+ players down | Remaining player(s) go stealth, hide for 5 min | 5 min wait |

{{< insight >}}The best teams treat every failed run as a learning opportunity. Review what went wrong: was the Infiltrator too slow? Did the Enforcer miss a guard? Did the Overwatch fail to spot? Each failure fixes a gap in the team's process.{{< /insight >}}
