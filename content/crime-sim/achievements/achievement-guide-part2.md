## Challenge Achievements (15)

Challenges are optional objectives that appear during runs. Completing them in specific quantities unlocks these achievements.

### Key "Challenges" Achievements

| Achievement | Requirement | Difficulty |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Insane victory | Complete 5 challenges in one game | Medium |
| Master challenger | Complete 10 challenges in one game | Hard |
| Challenge addict | Complete 25 challenges total | Medium |
| Speed demon | Complete 3 time-based challenges | Hard |
| Silent but deadly | Complete 3 stealth challenges | Medium |
| Heavy hitter | Complete 3 destruction challenges | Medium |
| All-rounder | Complete one of each challenge type | Very Hard |

### Challenge Types and How to Trigger

| Challenge Type | Trigger Condition | Example |
|----------------|------------------|---------|
| Time-based | Enter a house within a time limit | "Steal 3 items within 30 seconds" |
| Stealth | No detections during heist | "Complete house without being seen" |
| Destruction | Use force to enter | "Smash 3 windows in one heist" |
| Collection | Gather specific items | "Collect 5 electronics" |
| Tool mastery | Use specific tool set | "Open all doors with lockpicks" |

{{< callout "tip" >}}
For "All-rounder," use a large house with multiple approaches. The ground floor can be stealth, basement can be destruction, and upstairs can be time-based. One house can satisfy all challenge types.
{{< /callout >}}

## Speed Achievements (5)

These have strict time requirements.

| Achievement | Requirement | Strategy |
|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Speed runner | Complete a heist under 2 minutes | Choose a small apartment, grab 2 items, exit immediately |
| Quick getaway | Escape police within 30 seconds | Trigger a minor alert near a hideout spot |
| Speed looter | Steal 5 items in under 1 minute | Pre-scout the house layout, sprint past everything |
| Rush hour | Complete 3 heists in under 30 minutes | Chain small houses back to back |
| Delivery of a lifetime | Complete an item request within 1 minute of receiving it | Keep the requested item in your inventory, accept the request, deliver immediately |

For "Speed runner," use the starter tutorial area's nearest house. It is a small layout with minimal loot but maximum speed. Grab any 2 items and exit.

## Rare Condition Achievements (10)

These require unusual circumstances.

| Achievement | Requirement | How to Get |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Lucky break | Find a rare item in a common container | Check every container -- rare items spawn randomly |
| Three and free | Escape from a 3-star police chase | Trigger max police alert, escape successfully |
| Ghost | Complete a heist without leaving any evidence | Use lockpicks, no broken windows, no knocked-out NPCs |
| Tool breaker | Break 10 tools total | Use lower-quality tools deliberately |
| Millionaire | Accumulate $1,000,000 total across all runs | Pure grind in late game |
| Untouchable | Go 10 runs without arrest | Play exclusively safe houses |
| Night owl | Complete 5 heists at night | Use the day-night cycle |
| Early bird | Complete 3 heists before the quota timer starts | Sprint through easy houses after each quota reset |
| Furniture collector | Buy 15 pieces of hideout furniture | Requires significant cash investment in non-essentials |
| Delivery service | Complete 25 item requests total | Regular item request completion |

### "Three and Free" Strategy
1. Deliberately trigger police attention (smash a window in front of a patrol car)
2. Let your wanted level climb to 3 stars
3. Break line of sight and hide in a closet or dumpster
4. Stay hidden until the search timer de-escalates
5. Escape the area without getting arrested

This is easier with the Driver role or any speed boost skill.

{{< insight >}}
"Millionaire" is the grindiest achievement. At an estimated $2,000-$5,000 per hour in late-game, expect 40-60 hours of play. Completionist mode is recommended because uninterrupted heists are faster than quota-constrained ones.
{{< /insight >}}

## Completion Tips

- **Use Completionist mode** for all hunting -- no quota pressure
- **Stack achievements in single runs** -- one large house can satisfy 3-4 achievement requirements
- **Track your progress** -- Steam overlay shows achievement progress percentage
- **Backup saves** for Hard mode achievements -- copy your save file before attempting risky runs
