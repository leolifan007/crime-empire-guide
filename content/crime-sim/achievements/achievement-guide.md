{{< callout "info" >}}
Crime Simulator has 42 Steam achievements. Approximately 25 are earned through natural progression. The remaining 17 require specific challenges or hidden objectives. This guide covers every achievement with unlock conditions.

{{< /callout >}}

|-------------|-----------------|--------------|
| First Job | Complete your first job | 5 min |
| Rank Up (Rank 2) | Reach reputation rank 2 | 30 min |
| Rank Up (Rank 3) | Reach reputation rank 3 | 2 hrs |
| Rank Up (Rank 4) | Reach reputation rank 4 | 5 hrs |
| Rank Up (Rank 5) | Reach reputation rank 5 | 12 hrs |
| Big Spender | Spend $10,000 total | 4-6 hrs |

| Tooled Up | Own every tool type simultaneously | Mid-game |

| Tycoon | Accumulate $500,000 total income | 40-60 hrs |

{{< section "Stealth and Combat Achievements" >}}

| Achievement | Condition | Difficulty |
|-------------|-----------|------------|
| Ghost | Complete any job without being detected | Medium |
| Phantom | Complete 10 jobs without being detected | Hard |
| Shadow | Complete 25 jobs without being detected | Very Hard |
| Pacifist | Complete 5 jobs without harming anyone | Medium |
| First Blood | Eliminate your first target | Easy |
| One Shot | Eliminate 3 targets with headshots in one mission | Hard |
| Unstoppable | Survive 5 police pursuits in a row | Hard |

{{< insight >}}The "Shadow" achievement (25 clean jobs) is the hardest stealth challenge. One detection resets the counter. Night Vision Goggles, Stealth Movement 3, and Lockpicking 3 are essentially required. Plan every job around the "no detection" constraint -- skip any job with unavoidable guard contact.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Collector Achievement Item Locations" >}}

The "Collector" achievement requires finding 20 hidden statues scattered across the map:

| # | District | Location |
|---|----------|----------|
| 1-5 | Industrial Zone | Warehouse B office, Factory floor (2), Train yard (2) |
| 6-10 | Downtown | Bank lobby, Hotel room 304, Museum gift shop, Police station lobby, Parking garage |
| 11-15 | Suburbs | House #4 basement, House #7 attic, House #12 garage, Church bell tower, School gym |
| 16-20 | Luxury District | Mansion study (2), Pool house, Guard booth, Garden shed |
