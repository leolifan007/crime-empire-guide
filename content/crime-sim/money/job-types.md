{{< callout "info" >}}
Crime Simulator has 8 regular job types plus unique/high-value jobs. Each has a specific strategy, best loadout, and risk profile. This guide breaks down every mission type with pro strats for maximum profit per hour.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Regular Job Types" >}}

### 1. Steal Item (Best for Beginners)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Payout | $200-400 |
| Risk | Low |
| Loadout | Lockpick + crowbar |
| Best time | Any |

**Strategy:** These are simple fetch quests. Enter the marked building, find the item (glowing outline), grab it, leave. Police response only triggers if you break a window or are seen entering.

### 2. Break In

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Payout | $300-500 |
| Risk | Low-Medium |
| Loadout | Lockpick + flashlight |
| Best time | Night (02:00-05:00) |

**Strategy:** Enter a locked building without triggering the alarm. The alarm panel is always in one of 3 preset locations: behind the reception desk, in the back office, or near the emergency exit. Disarm it first (requires Lockpicking 1).

### 3. Steal Document

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Payout | $350-700 |
| Risk | Medium |
| Loadout | Lockpick + camera (to photograph documents) |
| Best time | Night |

**Strategy:** Similar to Steal Item but in higher-security locations. You need to photograph 2-3 documents from different rooms. Plan your route to minimize backtracking through guarded areas.

### 4. Loot Vehicle

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Payout | $150-350 |
| Risk | Low |
| Loadout | Crowbar (to break windows) |
| Best time | Night (02:00-06:00) |

**Strategy:** Break into parked cars and grab items from glovebox/trunk. Best done in residential areas where cars are plentiful. Each car takes 15 seconds. A full street yields $800-1,200 in under 10 minutes.

### 5. Debt Collection

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Payout | $400-800 |
| Risk | Medium |
| Loadout | Melee weapon (bat or pipe) |
| Best time | Any |

**Strategy:** Find the debtor, intimidate them. You have 3 approaches: threaten (requires Combatives 1), negotiate (requires Pickpocketing 1), or fight (requires weapon). Fighting is fastest but adds heat. Negotiation is slowest but clean.

### 6. Vandalism

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Payout | $200-400 |
| Risk | Very Low |
| Loadout | Spray can (from hardware store, $10) |
| Best time | Any |

**Strategy:** Tag 5 marked locations with spray paint. Quick, low-payout job good for filling gaps between bigger jobs. Can complete in 5 minutes.

### 7. Eliminate Target

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Payout | $600-1,500 |
| Risk | High |
| Loadout | Ranged weapon (pistol $500 or better) |
| Best time | Any, but crowded areas increase witness count |

**Strategy:** The highest-risk regular job. Must eliminate a named NPC without getting identified. Use a silenced weapon if possible. If using melee, ensure stealth kill. Witnesses = police ID = permanent heat in that district.

{{< section "Unique (High-Value) Jobs" >}}

Unique jobs appear on the board with a red border. They have special requirements:

| Job | Requirement | Payout | Special Rule |
|-----|------------|--------|-------------|
| Silent Extraction | Stealth Movement 2 | $2,000 | Cannot be seen at all |
| No Weapons Run | None | $1,500 | Carries no weapons, must improvise tools |
| Time Trial | Any | $1,500 | Complete within time limit (varies) |
| Target Rich | Combatives 3 | $3,000 | Must eliminate 3 targets in one location |
| Ghost Run | Lockpicking 3 | $2,500 | Cannot leave any trace (no broken locks) |

{{< insight >}}The "Ghost Run" unique job pays $2,500 but requires perfect execution -- any broken lock, broken window, or alarm trigger reduces payout to $500. It is the best test of your stealth skills. If you can complete a Ghost Run clean, you are ready for heists.{{< /insight >}}
