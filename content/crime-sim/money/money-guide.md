{{< callout "info" >}}
**Short answer:** The fastest money in Crime Simulator comes from a 3-phase progression: job board grinding ($200-500/hr early), then specialized heists ($1,500-3,000/hr mid), then passive income from hideouts ($5,000+/hr late).

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Phase 1: Job Board Grinding ($0 to $10,000)" >}}

The job board is your primary income source for the first 5-10 hours. Not all jobs are equal.

| Job Type | Base Pay | Time | Per Hour | Difficulty |
|----------|---------|------|----------|------------|
| Steal Item | $200-400 | 8-12 min | $1,500 | Easy |
| Break In | $300-500 | 10-15 min | $2,000 | Easy |
| Steal Document | $350-700 | 12-20 min | $2,100 | Medium |
| Loot Vehicle | $150-350 | 5-8 min | $1,500 | Easy |
| Debt Collection | $400-800 | 10-20 min | $2,400 | Medium |
| Vandalism | $200-400 | 5-10 min | $1,200 | Very Easy |
| Eliminate Target | $600-1,500 | 15-30 min | $3,000 | Hard |
| Unique Job | $1,000-3,000 | 20-45 min | $4,000 | Very Hard |

**The optimal job board loop:**

1. Only accept jobs in the same district (travel time eats profits)
2. Stack 2-3 jobs in the same area before traveling there
3. Complete them in order of difficulty (easiest first)
4. Return to hideout, accept new batch, repeat

{{< insight >}}The per-hour rates above assume perfect execution. If you trigger alarms, fight guards, or need to escape, each job takes 50% longer. Until you have Lockpicking 2 and Stealth Movement, add 30% to all time estimates.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Phase 2: Heists ($10,000 to $50,000)" >}}

At $10,000, you unlock the ability to plan heists -- multi-stage jobs that pay 5-10x more.

| Heist | Unlock Cost | Prep Time | Payout | Risk |
|-------|-----------|-----------|--------|------|
| Jewelry Store | $5,000 blueprint | 15 min | $8,000 | Medium |
| Bank Vault | $15,000 blueprint | 30 min | $25,000 | Very High |
| Museum Exhibit | $10,000 blueprint | 20 min | $15,000 | High |
| Corporate Office | $8,000 blueprint | 20 min | $12,000 | High |
| Police Station (evidence room) | $25,000 blueprint | 45 min | $40,000 | Extreme |

**Heist mechanics:** Each heist requires:
1. Purchase the blueprint ($5k-25k)
2. Gather tools (lockpicks, drills, masks, escape vehicle)
3. Scout the location (mark guard positions, camera blind spots)
4. Execute (follow plan, adapt to complications)
5. Escape (reach extraction point without arrest)

{{< callout "warning" >}}Do not attempt the Bank Vault or Police Station heist solo. The guard count (8-12) and alarm response time (30 seconds) make them nearly impossible without a teammate. These are designed for co-op.{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Phase 3: Passive Income ($50,000+)" >}}

| Passive Income Source | Initial Cost | Daily Payout | Breakeven |
|----------------------|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| Cannabis Farm (small) | $8,000 | $600/day | 14 days |
| Cannabis Farm (medium) | $25,000 | $2,000/day | 13 days |
| Cannabis Farm (large) | $75,000 | $6,000/day | 13 days |
| Protection Racket | $15,000 | $1,000/day | 15 days |
| Stolen Goods Fence | $20,000 | $1,500/day | 14 days |
| Crew Management | $50,000 | $4,000/day | 13 days |

**Best first passive investment:** Cannabis Farm (small). At $8,000, it pays back in 14 days and requires only a 5-minute daily harvest. The medium and large upgrades have slightly better ROI but need a larger upfront.
