{{< callout "info" >}}
Hardcore mode speedruns target reaching $100,000 net worth in under 10 hours of playtime. This is considered the ultimate Crime Simulator challenge. Only 3 known players have achieved it. Here is the route they used.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "The Optimal Hardcore Route (Hour-by-Hour)" >}}

### Hour 1: Survival Mode

| Objective | Method | Risk Level |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Complete 1 job | Loot Vehicle (easiest, $150-350) | Very Low |
| Buy Lockpick T1 + Crowbar | $150 total | -- |
| Complete 3 more jobs | Steal Item (suburban only) | Low |
| Buy Smoke Bomb x2 | $200 | -- |
| Complete 5 more jobs | Mix Loot Vehicle + Steal Item | Low |
| Buy Lockpick T2 | $300 | -- |

**End of hour 1:** 9 jobs completed, ~$1,500 net worth, basic gear.

### Hour 2: Safe Expansion

| Objective | Method | Risk Level |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Buy second hideout | Pawn shop stash ($500) | -- |
| Complete 5 Steal Item jobs | With Lockpick T2, easy | Low |
| Buy Night Vision | $1,200 (critical for night ops) | -- |
| Complete 3 night jobs | 2x payout, safer (fewer witnesses) | Low |
| Buy Lockpick T3 | $800 | -- |

**End of hour 2:** ~$4,000 net worth, Lockpick T3, Night Vision.

### Hour 3-4: Mid-Game Acceleration

| Objective | Method |
|-----------|--------|
| Complete 10 Break In jobs | Nighttime, Lockpick T3, $500+ each |
| Buy Silenced Pistol | $750 |
| Complete 5 Eliminate Target jobs | Silenced pistol = easy mode |
| Buy Armor Vest | $1,400 |
| Complete 3 more Break In jobs | Now armored, more aggressive |

**End of hour 4:** ~$15,000 net worth, silenced pistol, armor.

### Hour 5-8: High-Value Targets

| Objective | Method |
|-----------|--------|
| Complete 5 Unique Jobs | Red border jobs, $1,500-3,000 each |
| Buy Duffel Bag | $2,000 (40 slots) |
| Complete Jewelry Store heist | Solo possible with silenced pistol + stealth |
| Buy Car + Garage | $3,000 total |
| Complete Museum heist | Hard, but $15,000+ payout |
| Buy Cannabis Farm (small) | $8,000 passive income |

**End of hour 8:** ~$50,000 net worth, passive income running.

### Hour 9-10: Endgame Push

| Objective | Method |
|-----------|--------|
| Complete Bank Vault heist | Co-op if possible, solo if skilled |
| Buy Cannabis Farm (medium) | $25,000 |
| Complete Police Station heist | $40,000 payout |
| Final tally | $100,000+ net worth |

{{< section "Critical Decisions that Make or Break a Run" >}}

| Decision | Good Choice | Bad Choice | Consequence |
|----------|------------|------------|-------------|
| First 5 jobs | Loot Vehicle only | Break In | Alarm = restart run |
| Night vs Day | Always night ops | Day missions common | 3x witness risk |
| Lockpick skill order | T1 -> T2 -> T3 | Skip to T3 directly | Cannot open T2 locks to earn T3 money |
| Weapon buy order | Silenced pistol first | SMG or Shotgun | Loud weapons attract police swarm |
| First passive income | Cannabis Farm | Anything else | Farm is safest passive income |

{{< insight >}}The most common hardcore speedrun killer is overconfidence. Players hit $20k, buy expensive gear, attempt a heist, and die. The speedrun record-holders all report that strict adherence to the "no-heist-before-$50k" rule is what separates their runs from everyone else's.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Hardcore Permadeath Survival Tips" >}}

| Tip | Detail |
|-----|--------|
| Save every 15 minutes | Sleep at hideout. Set an alarm. |
| Never carry more than $3k | Lose it on death = 30 minute setback |
| Always carry 1 smoke bomb | It is your "get out of jail free" card |
| Know 3 escape routes per district | No exceptions |
| Do not trust fences | Some fences are police informants (random 5%) |
| Skip "Too Good" jobs | $3k payout on a simple job = trap |
| Never pick a fight with police | They arrive in waves. Win round 1, lose round 3. |

{{< section "Hardcore Tools Checklist" >}}

Before attempting any heist in hardcore, confirm:

- [ ] Lockpicking 3
- [ ] Stealth Movement 2+
- [ ] Silenced weapon
- [ ] Armor vest equipped
- [ ] Medkit x2 in inventory
- [ ] Smoke Bomb x2 in inventory
- [ ] EMP grenade x1
- [ ] Escape route memorized
- [ ] Backup hideout stocked with $2k bribe cash
- [ ] Vehicle parked within 100m of exit
