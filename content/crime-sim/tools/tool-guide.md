{{< section "Weapon Comparison" >}}

### Melee Weapons

| Weapon | Damage | Stealth Multiplier | Range | Cost | Unlock |
|--------|--------|-------------------|-------|------|--------|
| Fists | 15 | 1x | Short | Free | Default |
| Bat | 30 | 3x | Medium | $100 | Any |
| Pipe | 35 | 3x | Medium | $150 | Rank 2 |
| Knife | 40 | 5x | Short | $300 | Rank 3 |
| Machete | 55 | 4x | Long | $600 | Rank 4 |

**Best melee value:** The Bat ($100) is the optimal entry weapon. It one-shots civilians from stealth (30x3=90) and costs nothing compared to the knife ($300, only 10 more damage from stealth).

### Ranged Weapons

| Weapon | Damage | Range | Noise | Cost | Silent? |
|--------|--------|-------|-------|------|---------|
| Pistol | 40 | 30m | High | $500 | No |
| Silenced Pistol | 35 | 25m | Low | $750 | Yes |
| SMG | 25 | 40m | Very High | $1,500 | No |
| Silenced SMG | 22 | 35m | Medium | $2,500 | Yes |
| Hunting Rifle | 80 | 100m | Very High | $2,000 | No |
| Silenced Sniper | 75 | 90m | Low | $5,000 | Yes |

{{< callout "info" >}}The Silenced Pistol is the best weapon in the game for its price. It one-shots most NPCs from stealth at 25m, is quiet enough to use in multi-room buildings, and costs $750. Save up for it before any heist.{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Crafting System" >}}

At your hideout workbench, you can craft equipment:

| Crafted Item | Materials Needed | Time | Value |
|-------------|-----------------|------|-------|
| Lockpick (Tier 1) | 2x Scrap Metal | 30s | Saves $80 |
| Smoke Bomb | 1x Canister + 1x Chemicals | 2 min | Saves $60 |
| Silencer (pistol) | 3x Scrap Metal + 1x Rubber | 5 min | Saves $400 |
| Armor (vest) | 5x Scrap Metal + 2x Fiber | 15 min | Saves $800 |
| Lockpick (Tier 2) | 3x Scrap Metal + 1x Precision Tool | 5 min | Saves $200 |

**Crafting vs. Buying:** Crafting saves 40-60% over retail prices but costs time. During early game, buying is better (time is more valuable). Once you have passive income, crafting becomes worthwhile.

| Item | Craft Cost (materials) | Buy Cost | Savings |
|------|----------------------|----------|---------|
| Smoke Bomb | $40 | $100 | 60% |
| Silencer | $350 | $750 | 53% |
| Armor Vest | $600 | $1,400 | 57% |
| Lockpick T2 | $100 | $300 | 67% |
