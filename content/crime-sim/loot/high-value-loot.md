{{< section "Safe Locations and Combinations" >}}

Safes contain the highest-value loot but require knowing the combination or drilling.

| Safe Location | Contents | Lock Type | Combo Clue Location |
|---------------|----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Downtown Bank vault | $5,000 + random | Timed (30min) | Bank manager's phone (steal it first) |
| Jewellery Store back office | $3,000 + 2x Gold Watch | Keypad (4 digits) | Scrap of paper in break room |
| Museum Director's Office | $2,000 + Diamond Ring | Key (physical) | Director's pocket (pickpocket) |
| Mansion study | $4,000 + documents | Keypad (4 digits) | Painting behind = date of birth |
| Warehouse B safe | $1,500 + random loot | Mechanical dial | Note in shipping container #042 |

{{< insight >}}The Warehouse B safe is the easiest high-value safe. The combination is a mechanical dial (no electronics to trip). The clue note is always in Container #042, marked with a red X. Combination: 14 left, 32 right, 7 left.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Special Event Loot" >}}

Certain events cause temporary high-value loot spawns:

| Event | Trigger | Additional Loot | Value | Duration |
|-------|---------|----------------|-------|----------|
| Payday | Every Friday 17:00 | Cash on all NPCs x3 | +$200 per NPC | 2 hours |
| Holiday | In-game calendar | Gift boxes (random loot) | $500-2,000 each | 24 hours |
| Shipment Day | Every Monday | Warehouse B has 2x loot | $4,000+ | 8 hours |
| VIP Event | Random | Casino/Luxury district has high-value targets | $10,000+ | 4 hours |

{{< callout "warning" >}}Shipment Day is the most reliable special event (weekly). Plan your Monday mornings around hitting Warehouse B first thing. The loot despawns at the end of the 8-hour window, so do not delay.{{< /callover >}}

{{< section "Loot Transport Safety" >}}

Carrying high-value loot makes you a target for NPC thieves and police.

| Transport Method | Capacity | Security | Speed |
|-----------------|----------|---------|-------|
| On foot | Inventory | Low (you are the target) | Slow |
| Bicycle | Inventory +25% | Medium (difficult to follow) | Medium |
| Car trunk | 50 slots | High (locked trunk) | Fast |
| Delivery van | 100 slots | Very High (armored) | Fast |

**Best method:** Use a car for any loot run exceeding $3,000. The car trunk is locked (requires Lockpicking 3 for thieves to open) and gives you a quick escape option. Never walk through downtown carrying visible loot bags.
