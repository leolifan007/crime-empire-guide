Not all loot in Crime Simulator is worth carrying. With limited inventory space (15 slots base), you need to prioritize high-value items. A full inventory of $50 electronics wastes time that could be spent on a single $800 gold watch. Here is what to grab and what to leave.

{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Fencing (Selling Loot)" >}}

You cannot sell loot anywhere. It must go through the fence network:

| Fence Location | Types Accepted | Markup |
|----------------|---------------|--------|
| Pawn Shop (Pueblo) | Electronics, tools | 70% value |
| Street Dealer (Market) | Jewellery, watches | 85% value |
| Black Market (Docks) | All types | 90% value (best rate) |
| Online (Phone app) | High-value only ($500+) | 95% value (slow, 24hr) |

**Tips:**
- Always sell jewellery to the Street Dealer (85%)
- Always sell electronics to the Pawn Shop or Online
- Black Market has the best rates but requires reputation level 3
- Online sells for 95% but takes 24 hours to process
