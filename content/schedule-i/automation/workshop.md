Handler + Chemist + Packer — build the perfect AFK money-making production line.

## Automation Tiers

| Tier | Workers | Output/hr | Setup Cost | Profit/hr |
|------|---------|-----------|------------|-----------|
| Manual | 0 | 50g | $0 | $300 |
| Semi-auto | 2 | 200g | $8,000 | $1,200 |
| Full auto | 4 | 600g | $25,000 | $3,600 |

## Optimal Worker Setup

- **Handler**: Manages supplier relationships and bulk purchases
- **Chemist**: Automates mixing and quality control
- **Packer**: Handles packaging and distribution
- **Manager**: Oversees all operations (unlocked at level 12)

Place them in adjacent buildings to maximize efficiency bonus.
