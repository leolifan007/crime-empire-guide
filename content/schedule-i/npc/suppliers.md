{{< callout "info" >}}
**Short answer:** There are 3 core suppliers (Albert, Dan's Hardware, the hidden workshop) plus 2 special NPCs (Horse Semen vendor at the motel, and the meth precursor contact). Each is unlocked by ranking up and building their relationship bar through Dead Drop orders. The most commonly missed ingredient comes from a randomly-spawning NPC at the motel -- no map marker, no quest prompt.
{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Core Supplier Reference" >}}

| Supplier | Items Sold | Payment | Unlock Method | Location |
|----------|-----------|---------|---------------|----------|
| Albert | Seeds (all tiers), Banana, Cuke, basic ingredients | Cash + Dead Drop | Reach Hoodlum I, find him at motel | Motel room |
| Dan's Hardware | Mixing stations, shelves, equipment, Motor Oil, Gasoline | Cash | Reach Hustler II, enter hardware store | Hardware store downtown |
| Workshop (hidden) | Meth base, premium chemicals, Paracetamol | Cash only (no drops) | Reach Dealer I, find back-alley entrance | Industrial zone alley |

{{< section "How to Unlock Each Supplier" >}}

**Albert (Motel):** The easiest supplier to unlock. Find him in a room at the motel (east side of the map). Talk to him to start the relationship. Place Dead Drop orders to fill his relationship bar. Once full, he offers bulk pricing and better seeds.

**Dan's Hardware:** Walk into the hardware store downtown. Dan sells mixing equipment directly. Relationship bar increases with purchases. At full bar, Dan offers the best mixing station prices in the game.

**Hidden Workshop:** Located in the industrial zone, enter through an unmarked alley between two warehouses. Requires Dealer I rank to access. Sells meth precursor ingredients. Cash-only -- no Dead Drop option for this supplier.

{{< section "The Horse Semen NPC (Most Missed)" >}}

Horse Semen is NOT sold by a named supplier. It comes from a random NPC who can spawn in any motel room. The NPC appears after you reach Peddler I rank.

**How to find them:**
- Check every room at the motel
- The NPC has a unique model (not the standard dealer model)
- They offer Horse Semen and Mega Bean
- If not found, leave the area and return after 1 in-game hour
- The NPC respawns every in-game day

**Why it matters:** Horse Semen is the key ingredient for the OG Kush 4-mix that makes $136 profit per unit at Peddler III -- the best mid-game money-maker.

{{< section "Dead Drop Orders Explained" >}}

Dead Drop orders are how you increase a supplier's relationship bar. Select the supplier on your phone, choose Dead Drop, place an order for specific items, and a package appears at a designated location after 1-2 in-game hours.

| Supplier | Dead Drop Location | Delivery Time |
|----------|-------------------|---------------|
| Albert | Behind the motel dumpster | 1 hour |
| Dan's Hardware | Alley next to hardware store | 2 hours |
| Hidden Workshop | Inside the workshop back door | 1 hour |

Dead Drop orders cost the same as direct purchase but build relationship faster. Use Dead Drop exclusively when trying to unlock bulk pricing.

{{< section "Supplier Relationship Perks" >}}

Filling a supplier's relationship bar unlocks unique perks:

| Supplier | Full Bar Perk | Value |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| Albert | 20% discount on all seeds and ingredients | Saves $2-5 per order |
| Dan's Hardware | 15% discount on equipment | Saves $15-30 on mixing stations |
| Hidden Workshop | Access to premium chemicals | Unlocks advanced recipes |

The relationship bar fills faster with larger Dead Drop orders. A $200 order fills more bar than two $100 orders. Place infrequent but large orders to optimize your time.
