Heat maps, patrol patterns, safe-house placements — stay under the radar.

## Heat System

| Heat Level | Police Activity | Risk | Recommended Action |
|------------|----------------|------|-------------------|
| Low | Minimal patrols | Safe | Normal operations |
| Medium | Occasional checks | Moderate | Use safe houses |
| High | Active surveillance | Dangerous | Lay low 24hr |
| Maximum | Full crackdown | Extreme | Switch cities |

## Safe House Strategy

Maintain at least 3 safe houses across different districts. Rotate your operations base every 3 days to avoid pattern detection.
