{{< callout "info" >}}
**Short answer:** Your Heat Level rises when you sell drugs in public, get spotted by police while carrying contraband, or commit violent acts. Heat has 4 levels: Investigating (minor suspicion), Wanted (active chase), Wanted Dead or Alive (lethal force), and Maximum (city-wide lockdown). Heat decreases by staying out of sight -- hiding in a garbage bin for 30-60 seconds clears Wanted status completely.
{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "What Drives Heat Level Up" >}}

Your Heat Level is not a hidden number. It is a visible Wanted status that escalates based on player actions. The game uses a line-of-sight detection system -- police have to SEE you committing a crime to raise the level.

| Action | Heat Increase | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| Selling drugs to customers on the street | Low | Only if police witness the transaction |
| Carrying drugs/contraband in public | Low | Police spot you during patrol |
| Assaulting NPCs | Medium | Witnesses report to police |
| Car theft | Medium | Owner calls police |
| Using weapons in public | High | Immediate escalation |
| Resisting arrest | High | Automatically jumps to Wanted |
| Assaulting police officers | Maximum | Jumps to Wanted Dead or Alive |

{{< insight >}}The most common way players get heat early game is selling on the street in front of a police station or patrol route. Always check your minimap for the blue police icons before making a street sale. If you see a blue dot nearby, walk around the corner and sell there instead.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Heat Level Reference Table" >}}

| Level | Name | Police Behavior | Duration | How to Clear |
|-------|------|----------------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Investigating | Police walk toward your last known position | 30 seconds | Move 2 blocks away or enter a building |
| 2 | Wanted | Police chase on foot, use stun guns | 60 seconds | Break line of sight, hide in bin |
| 3 | Wanted Dead or Alive | Police use pistols, set up roadblocks | 90 seconds | Hide indoors or leave the district |
| 4 | Maximum | Full lockdown, police at every intersection | 3+ minutes | Switch properties or save and reload |
