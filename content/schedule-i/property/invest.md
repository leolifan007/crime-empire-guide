From broken RV to luxury mansion — the optimal property upgrade path for maximum ROI.

## Property Tiers

| Property | Cost | Capacity | Storage | Security | ROI Days |
|----------|------|----------|---------|----------|----------|
| RV | $2,000 | 50g | 100g | Low | 2 |
| Bungalow | $15,000 | 200g | 500g | Medium | 5 |
| Warehouse | $45,000 | 500g | 2,000g | High | 8 |
| Mansion | $120,000 | 2,000g | 10,000g | Max | 12 |

## Upgrade Timing

- **RV → Bungalow**: As soon as you have $15K. The extra capacity pays for itself.
- **Bungalow → Warehouse**: Wait until your daily volume exceeds 150g.
- **Warehouse → Mansion**: Only when you're moving 1,000g+/day.
