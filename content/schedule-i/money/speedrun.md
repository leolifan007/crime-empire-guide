{{< callout "info" >}}
**Short answer:** Deliveries and trash dumping are the safest early-game methods. A single optimized route: take a delivery order ($50-100 per delivery), grab trash on the way ($5-15 per dump), and smash vending machines ($20-50 each). Repeat for 1 hour = $8K-$12K with zero police risk. This is the fastest legal-ish money before unlocking dealers.
{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "The Optimal First-Hour Route" >}}

**Setup:** You need the Burned RV area as your base. Buy a trash can ($25 from Furniture shop) and place it at the RV. Grab a delivery order from your phone.

**Loop:**

1. Accept a delivery order (prefer ones close to the RV - check map distance)
2. Prepare the product (OG Kush from your first plant)
3. On the way to delivery, pick up any trash bags you see on the street
4. Complete the delivery for $50-$100
5. On the way back, smash any vending machines
6. Dump collected trash in your trash can at the RV
7. Repeat

| Loop # | Action | Time | Cumulative Earnings |
|--------|--------|------|-------------------|
| 1 | First harvest + delivery | 15 min | $500 |
| 2 | Delivery + trash pickup | 10 min | $700 |
| 3 | Two deliveries + vending | 15 min | $1,200 |
| 4 | Batch deliveries + trash | 20 min | $1,800 |

{{< insight >}}Always counter-offer on delivery prices. When a customer sends a deal through your phone, raise the price by 20-30%. Most customers accept. Overcharging on every deal adds $500+ per hour to your income.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Trash Dumping: Complete Guide" >}}

Trash bags are scattered throughout the map, especially near gas stations, behind buildings, and in alleyways. Each bag weighs 1 inventory slot.

| Trash Location | Bags Found | Restock Timer | Value |
|----------------|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Behind gas station (north) | 2-3 bags | Every in-game day | $5-10 each |
| Alley near apartment | 1-2 bags | Every in-game day | $8-15 each |
| Behind motel | 3-4 bags | Every in-game day | $5-12 each |
| Near dumpsters downtown | 2 bags | Every in-game day | $10-15 each |

You need a trash can at your property to dump the bags. The trash can costs $25 from the Furniture shop. Dumping trash does NOT generate police heat.

{{< section "Vending Machine Smashing" >}}

Vending machines in public areas can be smashed for cash and a free energy drink. Method: punch the machine 3-4 times. It breaks open and drops coins ($20-50).

**Best vending machines:** Gas station (north), near bleachers, at the parking lot. Avoid the one near the police station.

{{< insight >}}Combining all three methods (deliveries + trash + vending) can net you $10K in the first hour of play. This is enough to skip the grind and jump straight into mixing station setup at the Bungalow.{{< /insight >}}
