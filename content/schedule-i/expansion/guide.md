When to expand to new cities, which locations give best returns, and logistics optimization.

## City Comparison

| City | Entry Cost | Potential | Competition | Recommended Level |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| Starter City | $0 | Medium | High | 1-10 |
| Eastport | $25,000 | High | Medium | 10-18 |
| Westside | $50,000 | Very High | Low | 18-25 |
| Capital | $100,000 | Maximum | Medium | 25+ |

## Expansion Order

1. Dominate starter city first (80% market share)
2. Enter Eastport with a small outpost
3. Expand to Westside once you have $100K in liquid assets
4. Capital only after level 25

## Logistics

Maintain distribution centers every 2 cities. Each center covers a 100-mile radius. don't expand faster than your logistics network can support.
