{{< callout "info" >}}
**Short answer:** Schedule 1 does not have separate cities. The map is one interconnected town. "Expansion" means buying larger properties at Ray's Realty and unlocking supplier connections by building relationship bars. New areas become accessible as you rank up: Hoodlum opens the motel area, Hustler unlocks the dock zone, Dealer gives access to the warehouse district.
{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "Unlocking Progression by Rank" >}}

Your Threat Level (rank) determines which parts of the map are viable. Each rank unlocks new suppliers, properties, and customer types.

| Rank | Unlocks | New Area Access | Key Supplier |
|------|---------|----------------|--------------|
| Street Rat I-V | Basic delivery, first grow | Downtown + RV area | Albert Hoover (Northtown) |
| Hoodlum I-V | Mixing stations, Bungalow purchase | Westville | Albert Hoover |
| Hustler I-III | Botanist + Handler employees | Downtown | Shirley Watts, Fungal Phil |
| Peddler I-III | Horse Semen + Mega Bean supplies | Expanded area | Horseman NPC (motel random) |
| Dealer I-II | Meth production + Barn purchase | Industrial zone | All 4 suppliers available |
| Hoodlum V | Warehouse unlock | Docks (6PM-6AM) | Salvador Moreno |
| Kingpin | Manor + Cocaine 9-mix | All areas | All suppliers at max loyalty |

{{< section "Supplier Reference" >}}

Schedule 1 has 4 named suppliers, not including shops. Correct list:
- **Albert Hoover** (Northtown): Marijuana seeds, basic ingredients. Available from start.
- **Shirley Watts** (Westville): Pseudo for meth production. Unlock via Elizabeth Homley or Kevin Oakley.
- **Fungal Phil** (Downtown): Shroom spores and supplies. Same unlock connections as Shirley.
- **Salvador Moreno** (Docks): Coca plant seeds. Unlock at higher ranks.

Note that **Dan's Hardware** is a shop (not a supplier). The NPC behind the counter is **Dan Samwell**, a Shop Owner who sells equipment directly for cash -- no Dead Drop system.

{{< insight >}}Horse Semen is the most commonly missed ingredient. It comes from a random NPC at the motel -- not a named supplier with a map marker. Check every room at the motel. Missing this locks you out of the best mid-game OG Kush recipes.{{< /insight >}}

{{< section "Map Zones and Key Locations" >}}

| Zone | Key Locations | Police Activity | Best For |
|------|--------------|----------------|----------|
| Downtown (south) | Ray's Realty, Police Station | High | Property purchase, avoid selling |
| Motel area (east) | Suppliers, motel rooms | Low | Supplier runs, trash pickup |
| Industrial (north) | Barn, warehouse area | Medium | Automation setup |
| Docks (far east) | Docks Warehouse | Medium (night only) | Endgame production |
| Casino (north highway) | Casino, vending machines | Low | Gambling income |

{{< section "Zone Progression Path" >}}

1. **Start:** Downtown + RV area. Do delivery missions and first grow here.
2. **Early (Hoodlum):** Expand to the motel area. Buy Bungalow. Unlock Albert for better seeds.
3. **Mid (Hustler-Peddler):** Cover the industrial zone. Buy Barn. Unlock Horse Semen NPC.
4. **Late (Dealer-Hoodlum V):** Warehouse district at night. Automated production.
5. **Endgame (Kingpin):** All zones accessible. Manor operation.

{{< insight >}}The biggest expansion mistake is buying a property too far from your current supplier network. If your suppliers are downtown and your Barn is in the industrial zone, you will spend half your playtime running ingredients between zones. Only expand to a new zone AFTER you have unlocked a supplier in that zone.{{< /insight >}}
