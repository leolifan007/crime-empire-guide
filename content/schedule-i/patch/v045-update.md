{{< callout "info" >}}
**Short answer:** v0.4.5 (March 30, 2026) added the Golden M1911 pistol, Big Sprinkler for faster plant watering, a reorder feature for the delivery app, and dynamic reticle for ranged weapons. Meta impact: Big Sprinkler reduces Botanist workload by 40%, reorder saves 30% delivery management time. No major recipe or police behavior changes.
{{< /callout >}}

{{< section "New Additions in v0.4.5" >}}

| Feature | Type | Unlock | Impact |
|---------|------|--------|--------|
| Golden M1911 | Weapon | Casino vending machine (rare drop) | Cosmetic, same stats as M1911 |
| Big Sprinkler | Equipment | Dan's Hardware, $200 | Waters 4 plants at once -- huge automation upgrade |
| Reorder Feature | UI | Automatic | One-click repeat of last delivery order |
| Dynamic Reticle | UI | Automatic | Crosshair changes size based on accuracy |
| Bug reporting tool | System | Steam overlay | Report issues in-game |

{{< section "Big Sprinkler: The Real Game-Changer" >}}

The Big Sprinkler is the most impactful addition in v0.4.5. It replaces the basic sprinkler and covers a 4-plant area in one activation.

| Metric | Basic Sprinkler | Big Sprinkler |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Cost | $75 | $200 |
| Coverage | 1 plant | 4 plants |
| Water per use | 1 unit | 4 units |
| Botanist efficiency | 1 plant per pass | 4 plants per pass |
| Automation impact | Baseline | -40% Botanist labor time |

**Where to get it:** Sold at Dan's Hardware. No rank requirement to purchase.

{{< section "Meta Impact Analysis" >}}

**Before v0.4.5:** Botanists were the bottleneck in automation setups. One Botanist could water 5 pots but spent most of their time walking between pots. The basic sprinkler required one activation per plant.

**After v0.4.5:** One Big Sprinkler waters 4 plants. A single Botanist with 2 Big Sprinklers can now manage 8 plants (max for most properties) in the same time they previously managed 4-5.

**Other changes:**
- Reorder feature reduces delivery management time by ~30%
- No changes to police behavior, recipe values, or employee AI
- No new properties, suppliers, or dealers added

| Aspect | Pre-v0.4.5 | Post-v0.4.5 | Difference |
|--------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Botanist plants/hr | 5 | 8-10 | +60-100% |
| Daily plant yield (Barn) | 40 plants | 60-70 plants | +50-75% |
| Max automated units/day | ~200 | ~300 | +50% |

{{< section "What v0.4.5 Did NOT Change" >}}

Despite community speculation, the Anniversary update did NOT touch:
- Recipe profit values (OG Kush 4-mix still makes $136 profit)
- Police AI behavior (bin hiding still works)
- Property prices (Bungalow still $6K, Barn $25K)
- Employee wages (Chemists still $60/day)
- Dealer buy-in costs (no change to any dealer prices)
- Casino payout rates (Ride the Bus still +25% EV)

This makes v0.4.5 a quality-of-life patch rather than a balance patch. All existing strategies and guides remain valid.
