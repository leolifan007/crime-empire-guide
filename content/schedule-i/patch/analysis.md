New drugs, mechanics, and community vote results — everything that changed.

## New Content

- **3 New Strains**: Purple Haze, AK-47, White Widow
- **New Mechanic**: Cross-city logistics network
- **Quality of Life**: Batch processing for large orders

## Balance Changes

| Change | Before | After | Impact |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Green Crack yield | 150g/cycle | 120g/cycle | -20% |
| Blue Dream price | $45/g | $48/g | +6.7% |
| Warehouse cost | $45,000 | $38,000 | -15.6% |

## Meta Shift

The warehouse cost reduction makes property expansion more viable in early game. Focus on securing a warehouse before upgrading your grow operation.
