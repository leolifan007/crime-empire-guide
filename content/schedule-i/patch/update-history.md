{{< callout "info" >}}
**Short answer:** Schedule 1 launched in December 2024. Key milestones: v0.2.4 added co-op multiplayer, v0.3.4 was the first major content update, v0.4.0 stabilized multiplayer, and v0.4.5 (March 2026) added the Big Sprinkler and Golden M1911. The game is still in early access with regular patches every 2-4 months.

{{< section "What Each Major Version Changed" >}}

**v0.1.x (Launch):** Brought the core drug mixing and selling loop. Single zone map, basic suppliers, no employees. The game was functional but shallow.

**v0.2.4 (Multiplayer):** Added co-op for up to 4 players. This doubled the player base overnight. Suppliers and dealers now scale with player count.

**v0.3.4 (First Content Update):** Added functional stores (Dan's Hardware, Furniture shop, Convenience store), decor items for properties, and cosmetic options. This patch made the world feel alive.

**v0.4.0 (Stability):** Focused on fixing multiplayer desync issues. Not a content patch, but critical for co-op players.

**v0.4.5 (Anniversary Update):** The biggest quality-of-life patch. Big Sprinkler (4-plant watering) changed automation meta. Reorder feature saved delivery management time. Golden M1911 is purely cosmetic.
