{{< section "What Each Major Version Changed" >}}

**v0.1.x (Launch, Mar 2025):** Core drug mixing and selling loop. Single zone map, basic suppliers, no employees. The game was functional but shallow.

**v0.2.4 (Multiplayer, Apr 2025):** Added co-op for up to 4 players. Expanded the player base significantly. Suppliers and dealers now scale with player count.

**v0.3.4 (First Content Update, Apr 7 2025):** Added functional stores (Pawn Shop, Dan's Hardware, Furniture shop), decor items for properties, and Fancy Stuff cosmetics. First major content expansion.

**v0.4.0 (Cartel Update, Aug 28 2025):** The Benzies Family Cartel update -- major content drop with new mechanics, items, and story elements.

**v0.4.1 (Halloween Update, Nov 2 2025):** Seasonal content with Halloween-themed items and decorations.

**v0.4.2 (Shrooms Update, Dec 26 2025):** Added Fungal Phil as a new supplier, shroom-based production lines, and related mechanics.

**v0.4.5 (Anniversary Update, Mar 30 2026):** Big Sprinkler (4-plant watering) changed automation meta. Reorder feature saved delivery management time. Golden M1911 is purely cosmetic. Dynamic reticle added for ranged weapons.

{{< section "What Players Want in Future Updates" >}}

Based on Reddit (r/Schedule_I) and Steam Community discussions, the most requested features include new map expansion, police raid system, and more employee types. The developer TVGS has not announced a specific roadmap.
