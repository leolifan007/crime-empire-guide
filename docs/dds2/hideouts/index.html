DDS2 Hideout Locations Guide and Raid Protection

Jun 9, 2026
Hideouts in DDS2 can be raided based on your wanted level and the hideout's visibility. The community guide "Pedro Alvarez Real Estate Agency" documents all locations with prices, water/phone line availability, and visibility ratings. Here is what you need to know.

How Raids Work

The DDS2 Fandom hideout page confirms:

  • Raid probability is affected by wanted level (None / Person of Interest / Suspect / Wanted)
  • Hideout visibility is a factor -- highly visible locations attract more attention
  • Helicopters can appear during raids
  • A single hideout with all your assets is a high-risk strategy
Wanted LevelRaid ProbabilityProtection Strategy
NoneLowStandard precautions
Person of InterestMediumSplit assets, 2+ hideouts
SuspectHighGuards, hidden compartments
WantedVery HighMinimize time at any hideout

Community consensus from r/DrugDealerSimOfficial: If you turn off raids (game settings), you can use any location for space. With raids enabled, prioritize low-visibility locations for your main lab.

Protection Checklist

  • [ ] Own at least 2 hideouts before your net worth exceeds $5,000
  • [ ] Keep raw materials and finished product in separate locations
  • [ ] Maintain low wanted level before visiting high-value hideouts
  • [ ] Use rural/jungle hideouts for your main lab (lower visibility)
  • [ ] When raided, escape using parkour rather than fighting

DDS2 Raid Prevention: Protecting Your Assets

Jun 9, 2026
Getting raided in DDS2 is preventable. The game's raid system checks wanted level and hideout visibility. Keep both low and your assets will stay safe. Here is the verified strategy from community guides.

Raid Cause and Effect

FactorImpact on Raid ChanceHow to Control
Wanted levelDirect multiplierAvoid illegal activity in populated areas
Hideout visibilityLocation-dependentChoose rural/jungle hideouts for labs
Product volumeIndirect (attracts attention)Split across locations
Guard presenceReduces probabilityHire guards when available

Asset Protection Strategy

Three-hideout model (community standard):

HideoutStoresRisk if Raided
A (rural)Raw materials, low-value productLow -- easy to replace
B (industrial)Processing equipment, work-in-progressMedium -- loss of time
C (coastal)Finished product, cashHigh -- biggest value

If one hideout gets raided, you lose at most one-third of your operation.

What to Do During a Raid

Per the Fandom wiki: raids can involve helicopters and heavily armed police. Your options:

  1. Escape -- Use parkour to break line of sight
  2. Hide -- Some hideouts have hiding spots
  3. Fight -- Not recommended (police arrive in waves)
  4. Accept loss -- If you have split assets, the damage is contained
Community consensus: Fighting during a raid increases your wanted level and makes future raids more likely. Escape is always the better option.
