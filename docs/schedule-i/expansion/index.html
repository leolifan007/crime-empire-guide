Map Expansion Guide: Unlocking New Areas and Supplier Routes

Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: Schedule 1 does not have separate cities. The map is one interconnected town. "Expansion" means buying larger properties at Ray's Realty and unlocking supplier connections by building relationship bars. New areas become accessible as you rank up: Hoodlum opens the motel area, Hustler unlocks the dock zone, Dealer gives access to the warehouse district.

Unlocking Progression by Rank

Your Threat Level (rank) determines which parts of the map are viable. Each rank unlocks new suppliers, properties, and customer types.

Supplier Reference

Schedule 1 has 4 named suppliers, not including shops. Correct list:

  • Albert Hoover (Northtown): Marijuana seeds, basic ingredients. Available from start.
  • Shirley Watts (Westville): Pseudo for meth production. Unlock via Elizabeth Homley or Kevin Oakley.
  • Fungal Phil (Downtown): Shroom spores and supplies. Same unlock connections as Shirley.
  • Salvador Moreno (Docks): Coca plant seeds. Unlock at higher ranks.

Note that Dan's Hardware is a shop (not a supplier). The NPC behind the counter is Dan Samwell, a Shop Owner who sells equipment directly for cash -- no Dead Drop system.

Horse Semen is the most commonly missed ingredient. It comes from a random NPC at the motel -- not a named supplier with a map marker. Check every room at the motel. Missing this locks you out of the best mid-game OG Kush recipes.

Zone Progression Path

  1. Start: Downtown + RV area. Do delivery missions and first grow here.
  2. Early (Hoodlum): Expand to the motel area. Buy Bungalow. Unlock Albert for better seeds.
  3. Mid (Hustler-Peddler): Cover the industrial zone. Buy Barn. Unlock Horse Semen NPC.
  4. Late (Dealer-Hoodlum V): Warehouse district at night. Automated production.
  5. Endgame (Kingpin): All zones accessible. Manor operation.

The biggest expansion mistake is buying a property too far from your current supplier network. If your suppliers are downtown and your Barn is in the industrial zone, you will spend half your playtime running ingredients between zones. Only expand to a new zone AFTER you have unlocked a supplier in that zone.

Map Zones and Key Locations: Complete Area Guide

Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: The Schedule 1 map has 5 main zones: Downtown, Motel Area, Industrial Zone, Docks, and Highway (Casino). Each zone has different suppliers, police density, and available properties. Downtown and Motel are safe for early game. Industrial and Docks are mid-to-late game zones. The Highway zone has the casino and is most active at night.

Downtown Zone

The starting area. Contains:

  • Ray's Realty (northeast, near Garage and Bleuball's Boutique). Buy all properties here.
  • Police Station (south end). Avoid selling within 3 blocks.
  • Dan's Hardware (central). Equipment and mixing stations.
  • Convenience Store (central). Donuts and basic items.
  • Gas Station (north edge). Fuel, Energy Drinks, basic ingredients.

Selling strategy: High police density makes downtown risky for street dealing. Use this zone for shopping and property purchases only. Do your street sales in the Motel area.

Motel Area (East)

The best early-game zone. Contains:

  • Albert Hoover: primary mid-tier supplier, Motel room
  • Random NPC rooms: Horse Semen vendor (check every room)
  • Trash spawns: 3-4 bags behind motel, restock daily
  • Parking garage: rooftop hiding spot (police escape)

Selling strategy: Lowest police density in the game. Perfect for street dealing and dealer assignments. Buy the Bungalow here first.

Industrial Zone (North)

Mid-game zone, unlocked at Dealer rank. Contains:

  • The Barn (east side): $25K, best value property
  • Shirley Watts' stash: West face of Warehouse
  • Fungal Phil's stash: Behind Gas-Mart

Selling strategy: Medium police density. Use dealers here instead of street selling. The Barn's 2-floor layout takes time to optimize.

Docks (Far East)

Late-game zone, accessible 6PM-6AM. Contains:

  • Docks Warehouse ($50K): largest pre-Manor property
  • Salvador Moreno's stash: Behind Church (east side)

Selling strategy: Night-only access means you need to plan your production schedule around the time gate. Stockpile ingredients during the day and produce at night.

Highway/Casino Zone (North)

The northern highway has the casino and several vending machines. No properties available here. Best for gambling runs and quick cash via vending machines.
