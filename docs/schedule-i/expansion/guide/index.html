Map Expansion Guide: Unlocking New Areas and Supplier Routes

Updated: Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: Schedule 1 does not have separate cities. The map is one interconnected town. "Expansion" means buying larger properties at Ray's Realty and unlocking supplier connections by building relationship bars. New areas become accessible as you rank up: Hoodlum opens the motel area, Hustler unlocks the dock zone, Dealer gives access to the warehouse district.

Supplier Reference

Schedule 1 has 4 named suppliers, not including shops. Correct list:

  • Albert Hoover (Northtown): Marijuana seeds, basic ingredients. Available from start.
  • Shirley Watts (Westville): Pseudo for meth production. Unlock via Elizabeth Homley or Kevin Oakley.
  • Fungal Phil (Downtown): Shroom spores and supplies. Same unlock connections as Shirley.
  • Salvador Moreno (Docks): Coca plant seeds. Unlock at higher ranks.

Note that Dan's Hardware is a shop (not a supplier). The NPC behind the counter is Dan Samwell, a Shop Owner who sells equipment directly for cash -- no Dead Drop system.

Horse Semen is the most commonly missed ingredient. It comes from a random NPC at the motel -- not a named supplier with a map marker. Check every room at the motel. Missing this locks you out of the best mid-game OG Kush recipes.

Zone Progression Path

  1. Start: Downtown + RV area. Do delivery missions and first grow here.
  2. Early (Hoodlum): Expand to the motel area. Buy Bungalow. Unlock Albert for better seeds.
  3. Mid (Hustler-Peddler): Cover the industrial zone. Buy Barn. Unlock Horse Semen NPC.
  4. Late (Dealer-Hoodlum V): Warehouse district at night. Automated production.
  5. Endgame (Kingpin): All zones accessible. Manor operation.

The biggest expansion mistake is buying a property too far from your current supplier network. If your suppliers are downtown and your Barn is in the industrial zone, you will spend half your playtime running ingredients between zones. Only expand to a new zone AFTER you have unlocked a supplier in that zone.
