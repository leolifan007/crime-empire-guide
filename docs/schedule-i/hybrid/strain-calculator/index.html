Strain Mix Calculator: Ingredient Chain Optimization

Updated: Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: Use the Schedule 1 Calculator (schedule-1-calculator.com) for real-time ingredient chain analysis. The optimal mixing strategy: add ingredients in ascending value order. Each ingredient contributes different stat multipliers -- Battery adds potency, Cuke adds stability, Donut adds random effects. A 4-ingredient mix is the sweet spot for profit per station.

Calculator Strategy

The online calculator (schedule-1-calculator.com) lets you test ingredient combinations before spending in-game money. Here is how to use it effectively:

  1. Select your base ingredient (determines strain category)
  2. Add ingredients one at a time, noting the sell price change
  3. Reorder ingredients to see price variation (order matters!)
  4. Find the combination with the highest sell price for 4-5 ingredients
  5. Verify all ingredients are available at your current rank

The calculator reveals a counter-intuitive fact: sometimes dropping an expensive ingredient increases final value. This happens when an ingredient's effect conflicts with the base strain's natural properties. Always test 3-4 different combinations -- the highest ingredient-cost recipe is NOT always the highest sell price.
