Mixing Mania $60 Quest: Step-by-Step Completion

Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: The Mixing Mania quest asks you to create a strain worth $60 using a Mixing Station from Dan's Hardware. The recipe: OG Kush + Cuke + Banana + Donut = $68 strain. Buy OG Kush seeds from Albert, Cuke and Banana from gas station or Albert, Donut from the convenience store. Mix in this exact order at your Mixing Station.

Mixing Mania Quest Step by Step

1
Buy a Mixing Station $100

Go to Dan's Hardware (downtown) and buy a Mixing Station for $100. Place it in your apartment or Bungalow. You also need a Mixing Station Clipboard ($25) to configure the recipe.

2
Gather Ingredients 4 Items

You need: OG Kush (buy seeds from Albert at the motel, grow or buy directly), Cuke (sold at gas station or from Albert), Banana (sold at gas station or from Albert), Donut (sold at the convenience store near downtown). Total ingredient cost: ~$12.

3
Mix in Exact Order OG Kush Base

Place OG Kush in the Mixing Station as the base ingredient. Then add Banana, then Cuke, then Donut, in that sequence. Each ingredient adds different property boosts. The order matters for the final strain value.

4
Collect Your $68 Strain Quest Complete

The finished product should be worth $68. Sell it to a customer for the quest completion reward. You also unlock the ability to create custom strains at your Mixing Station permanently.

When to Start Mixing Custom Strains

Custom strain mixing becomes profitable once you have a Mixing Station and access to 4+ ingredient types. The best time to start is at Hustler rank when you have unlocked Albert (motel) for mid-tier seeds.

Early game (Hustler-Peddler): Stick to 4-ingredient OG Kush mixes. The Triple Dollar recipe ($120 profit) funds your Bungalow purchase faster than selling raw product.

Mid game (Dealer-Hoodlum): Transition to Horse Semen + Mega Bean combos. The Gorilla Mix ($150+) pays for employee wages while you save for the Barn.

Late game (Kingpin): Cocaine 9-mix blows all custom strains out of the water at $717 net per unit. Use custom mixing only for niche effects or specific customer requests.

Calculator Strategy

The online calculator (schedule-1-calculator.com) lets you test ingredient combinations before spending in-game money. Here is how to use it effectively:

  1. Select your base ingredient (determines strain category)
  2. Add ingredients one at a time, noting the sell price change
  3. Reorder ingredients to see price variation (order matters!)
  4. Find the combination with the highest sell price for 4-5 ingredients
  5. Verify all ingredients are available at your current rank

The calculator reveals a counter-intuitive fact: sometimes dropping an expensive ingredient increases final value. This happens when an ingredient's effect conflicts with the base strain's natural properties. Always test 3-4 different combinations -- the highest ingredient-cost recipe is NOT always the highest sell price.
