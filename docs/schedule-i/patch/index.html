Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: v0.4.5 (March 30, 2026) added the Golden M1911 pistol, Big Sprinkler for faster plant watering, a reorder feature for the delivery app, and dynamic reticle for ranged weapons. Big Sprinkler: The Real Game-Changer

The Big Sprinkler is the most impactful addition in v0.4.5. It replaces the basic sprinkler and covers a 4-plant area in one activation.

Where to get it: Sold at Dan's Hardware. No rank requirement to purchase.

Meta Impact Analysis

Before v0.4.5: Botanists were the bottleneck in automation setups. One Botanist could water 5 pots but spent most of their time walking between pots. The basic sprinkler required one activation per plant.

After v0.4.5: One Big Sprinkler waters 4 plants. A single Botanist with 2 Big Sprinklers can now manage 8 plants (max for most properties) in the same time they previously managed 4-5.

Other changes:

  • Reorder feature reduces delivery management time by ~30%
  • No changes to police behavior, recipe values, or employee AI
  • No new properties, suppliers, or dealers added

What Each Major Version Changed

v0.1.x (Launch, Mar 2025): Core drug mixing and selling loop. Single zone map, basic suppliers, no employees. The game was functional but shallow.

v0.2.4 (Multiplayer, Apr 2025): Added co-op for up to 4 players. Expanded the player base significantly. Suppliers and dealers now scale with player count.

v0.3.4 (First Content Update, Apr 7 2025): Added functional stores (Pawn Shop, Dan's Hardware, Furniture shop), decor items for properties, and Fancy Stuff cosmetics. First major content expansion.

v0.4.0 (Cartel Update, Aug 28 2025): The Benzies Family Cartel update -- major content drop with new mechanics, items, and story elements.

v0.4.1 (Halloween Update, Nov 2 2025): Seasonal content with Halloween-themed items and decorations.

v0.4.2 (Shrooms Update, Dec 26 2025): Added Fungal Phil as a new supplier, shroom-based production lines, and related mechanics.

v0.4.5 (Anniversary Update, Mar 30 2026): Big Sprinkler (4-plant watering) changed automation meta. Reorder feature saved delivery management time. Golden M1911 is purely cosmetic. Dynamic reticle added for ranged weapons.

What Players Want in Future Updates
