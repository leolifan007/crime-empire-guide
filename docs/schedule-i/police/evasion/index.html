How to Escape Cops: Garbage Bin Hiding + Line of Sight Break

Updated: Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: Run behind a building to break line of sight, then hide in a garbage bin for 30-60 seconds. That clears any Wanted level. If no bin is nearby, enter a safe house or use an Energy Drink speed boost to outrun the pursuing officers. Police pathfinding is weak -- they struggle with jumping over fences and navigating through buildings.

Step-by-Step: The Bin Escape

1
Run Immediately First Step

As soon as you see the red Wanted indicator, sprint away from the police officer. Do not stop to stash items or fight -- every second counts. Run perpendicular to the officer's approach direction, not straight away.

2
Break Line of Sight Crucial

Duck behind the nearest large object: a building, a fence, a dumpster, or a parked truck. Police rely on visual contact. Once you disappear from their view for 3-5 seconds, they stop sprinting and go into search mode. This is your window to hide.

Find and Enter a Garbage Bin The Trick

Bins are located behind most buildings, in alleyways, and near gas stations. Walk up to a bin and interact to climb inside. Wait 30-45 seconds while the Wanted timer depletes. Do NOT leave the bin early -- if the timer is still active, police will immediately reacquire you.

4
Exit and Move to Safe Zone Recovery

Once the Wanted indicator disappears, exit the bin. Move to a different district immediately. Police patrol density increases temporarily in the area where you were chased. Wait 1 in-game hour before returning.

5
Advanced: The Parking Garage Roof Guaranteed Safe

The parking garage rooftop near the motel is a guaranteed hiding spot. Police pathfinding cannot reach the roof. Run up the ramp, jump to the top level, and wait. This works at any Heat Level and is the only 100% reliable escape location in v0.4.5.

If the Bin Method Fails

The bin method sometimes fails if a police officer saw you enter the bin. Here are backup options:

A common myth is that dumping your drugs removes the Wanted level. This is false -- once police have seen you commit a crime, dropping items does not stop the chase. Focus on running and hiding, not on stash management.

What NOT To Do When Wanted

  • Do not fight police -- attacking an officer jumps you to Wanted Dead or Alive immediately
  • Do not drive through the main street -- police roadblocks appear at higher levels
  • Do not return to your property while Wanted -- police can follow you inside in some patches
  • Do not assume the chase is over just because you lost sight -- police search the area for 60 seconds after losing you
