Heat Levels Explained: What Triggers Police and How to Reduce It

Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: Your Heat Level rises when you sell drugs in public, get spotted by police while carrying contraband, or commit violent acts. Heat has 4 levels: Investigating (minor suspicion), Wanted (active chase), Wanted Dead or Alive (lethal force), and Maximum (city-wide lockdown). Heat decreases by staying out of sight -- hiding in a garbage bin for 30-60 seconds clears Wanted status completely.

What Drives Heat Level Up

Your Heat Level is not a hidden number. It is a visible Wanted status that escalates based on player actions. The game uses a line-of-sight detection system -- police have to SEE you committing a crime to raise the level.

The most common way players get heat early game is selling on the street in front of a police station or patrol route. Always check your minimap for the blue police icons before making a street sale. If you see a blue dot nearby, walk around the corner and sell there instead.

How to Reduce Heat Fast

1
Break Line of Sight Instant

Run behind a building, fence, or into an alleyway. Police pathfinding is poor at tracking you through terrain. Once you are out of visual contact for 3-5 seconds, the Wanted timer starts ticking. Jump over low walls and fences to create distance quickly.

Hide in a Garbage Bin Best Method

Garbage bins are scattered throughout the map. Walk up to one and interact to hide inside. After 30-45 seconds, the Wanted timer expires completely. Note: police can still find you if they see you enter the bin. Do not hide in the same bin twice in a row -- cops learn your hiding spots.

3
Use Energy Drink to Outrun Speed Boost

Consuming an Energy Drink gives a temporary speed boost. Use it when fleeing to create distance faster. Combine with line-of-sight breaks for the most reliable escape. Energy Drinks are sold at gas stations for $5 each.

4
Enter a Safe House Full Clear

Entering any owned property instantly clears all Heat Level. This is the most reliable escape method if you are close to a safe house. Keep at least one property near your primary selling district for quick escapes.

5
Avoid Getting Caught Altogether Prevention

Store drugs in your property, not on your person, when not selling. Keep illegal items in alternate inventory slots so police searches find nothing. Do not sell within 3 blocks of the police station. Use dealers to sell for you instead of street dealing -- dealers do not trigger Heat.

How to Escape Cops: Garbage Bin Hiding + Line of Sight Break

Jun 9, 2026
Short answer: Run behind a building to break line of sight, then hide in a garbage bin for 30-60 seconds. That clears any Wanted level. If no bin is nearby, enter a safe house or use an Energy Drink speed boost to outrun the pursuing officers. Police pathfinding is weak -- they struggle with jumping over fences and navigating through buildings.

Step-by-Step: The Bin Escape

1
Run Immediately First Step

As soon as you see the red Wanted indicator, sprint away from the police officer. Do not stop to stash items or fight -- every second counts. Run perpendicular to the officer's approach direction, not straight away.

2
Break Line of Sight Crucial

Duck behind the nearest large object: a building, a fence, a dumpster, or a parked truck. Police rely on visual contact. Once you disappear from their view for 3-5 seconds, they stop sprinting and go into search mode. This is your window to hide.

Find and Enter a Garbage Bin The Trick

Bins are located behind most buildings, in alleyways, and near gas stations. Walk up to a bin and interact to climb inside. Wait 30-45 seconds while the Wanted timer depletes. Do NOT leave the bin early -- if the timer is still active, police will immediately reacquire you.

4
Exit and Move to Safe Zone Recovery

Once the Wanted indicator disappears, exit the bin. Move to a different district immediately. Police patrol density increases temporarily in the area where you were chased. Wait 1 in-game hour before returning.

5
Advanced: The Parking Garage Roof Guaranteed Safe

The parking garage rooftop near the motel is a guaranteed hiding spot. Police pathfinding cannot reach the roof. Run up the ramp, jump to the top level, and wait. This works at any Heat Level and is the only 100% reliable escape location in v0.4.5.

If the Bin Method Fails

The bin method sometimes fails if a police officer saw you enter the bin. Here are backup options:

BackupHow ToReliability

Reload saveQuit to menu and reload -- police reset to default positions100% (last resort)

A common myth is that dumping your drugs removes the Wanted level. This is false -- once police have seen you commit a crime, dropping items does not stop the chase. Focus on running and hiding, not on stash management.

What NOT To Do When Wanted

  • Do not fight police -- attacking an officer jumps you to Wanted Dead or Alive immediately
  • Do not drive through the main street -- police roadblocks appear at higher levels
  • Do not return to your property while Wanted -- police can follow you inside in some patches
  • Do not assume the chase is over just because you lost sight -- police search the area for 60 seconds after losing you
